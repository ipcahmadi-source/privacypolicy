Privacy Policy for IPC Ahmadi Kuwait Church App

This application is developed and maintained by IPC Ahmadi Kuwait Church for the exclusive use of its church members.

We do not collect, share, or sell any personal data from users.
Only pre-created usernames and passwords are used to allow secure access for church members.

The app uses Firebase services for displaying church announcements, daily Bible verses, and push notifications. These services may automatically collect limited technical information such as device tokens for delivering notifications. This data is managed securely by Google Firebase and is not used for any other purpose.

No user analytics, tracking, advertisements, or third-party integrations are included in this app.
